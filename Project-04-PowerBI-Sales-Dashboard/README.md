## üìà Dashboard Components

### KPI Cards
- **Total Revenue:** $899.9M total sales
- **Total Profit:** Operating profit metrics
- **Units Sold:** Total units sold across all products

### Visualizations

**1. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)**
- Time series analysis of revenue
- Identifies seasonal patterns
- Tracks growth trajectory

**2. Revenue by Product (Bar Chart)**
- Product category performance
- Top-selling products identification
- **Key Finding:** Men's Street Footwear leads sales

**3. Revenue by State (Column Chart)**
- Geographic sales distribution
- State-level performance
- **Key Finding:** New York is top-performing state

**4. Sales by Channel (Donut Chart)**
- Sales method comparison (In-store, Online, Outlet)
- Channel performance analysis
- **Key Finding:** In-store sales dominate

### Interactive Filters (Slicers)
- **Date Filter:** Dynamic date range selection
- **Region Filter:** Northeast, Southeast, South, West, Midwest
- **City Filter:** City-level filtering
